HOW DOES IT WORK?

A doctor will be sitting at the other end who will diagnose your problems and try to recommend the ideal treatment. The doctor may ask you for additional information to confirm his diagnosis. Prescription will be provided where required (as a scanned copy). In case diagnosis can not be confirmed due to technology or doctor’s limitations, the doctor will mention the same and urge you to consult the right specialist.

FAQ

  1. I can't see the chat!

    2. The chat is powered by tawk.to and should be visible within a few seconds of loading this page. If you can't see the chat, try to access the site from another network (chat applications are at times blocked by office firewalls). Otherwise, reach out to us at indoclinichealthcare@gmail.com. We would be glad to help.

  2. How do I know I am not talking to a robot ?

    3. The doctor will mention his name and degree. Technology is not advanced enough for a robot to make a correct diagnosis (yet).

  3. When do I pay?

    4. You do not pay. It's free.

  4. Is it only over chat ?

    5. If the doctor feels the need to talk to you to confirm the diagnoses or if you need further clarification on the consultation provided, it can be taken up over the phone. However our doctors are busy, and phone should be used as a last resort

  5. What if something goes wrong?

    6. Our doctors are trained to turn away patients where an ‘in-person’ consultation is required. Hence chances of wrong diagnoses are minimum as long as the patient discloses correct information about his/her condition. However please read our Terms and Conditions before proceeding. Incase of an emergency, please call the nearest hospital

  6. Can I join as a doctor?

    7. We would love to have more doctors. Hit the ‘Join Us’ button at the top of the site and fill in the form. We will get back to you asap.

  7. Who are you?

    8. We are a start-up trying to discover inefficiencies in the healthcare sector. You can reach us at indoclinichealthcare@gmail.com
