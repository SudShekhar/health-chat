HOW DOES IT WORK?

A doctor will be sitting at the other end who will diagnose your problems and try to recommend the ideal treatment. The treating doctor may ask for additional information to confirm his diagnosis. In case diagnosis can not be confirmed due to technology or doctor’s limitations, the doctor will mention the same and urge you to consult the right specialist
