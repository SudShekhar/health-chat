Indoclinic

  1. The doctor has taken the relevant inputs to the best of his knowledge and done relevant examination under the limitation of telemedicine
  2. Patient has given correct information about himself / herself
  3. In case of disease affecting the private parts, patient should send pictures of only the affected parts (excluding face) to ensure privacy
  4. Patient should not conceal any information about previous diseases, drugs taken , pregnancy condition , previous surgery etc. which can have an impact on the diagnoses and treatment
  5. Patient consent will be taken for use of any drugs whose use is associated with serious but rare side effects or is contraindicated in pregnancy, lactation, or old age or in children.
  6. Only FDA approved drugs will be prescribed as first line treatment but in exceptional cases off label used of drugs can be prescribed. Patient should know that treatment is prescribed in good faith
  7. If patient develops any immediate side effects to the medication, he should consult the doctor immediately.
  8. Patient should buy the drugs from the prescribed brands or of reputed brands if the prescribed brands are not available. The doctor will prescribe the best possible drugs available in the market, however in exceptional condition patient can develop side effects due to unknown circumstances
  9. Patient should follow up with the doctor at the prescribed time.
  10. Patient should know that taking wrong dosages of drugs can lead to serious side effect and may lead to treatment failure.
  11. If patient feels that doctor was unable to understand his complaints and was unable to give treatment to the satisfaction of the patient , he/she is free to consult any other doctor.
  12. This service is being done in best interest of patients
